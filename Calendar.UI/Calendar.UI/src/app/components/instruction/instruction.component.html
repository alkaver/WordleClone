How to Play Wordle

Welcome to Wordle! The goal of the game is to guess a hidden 5-letter word within 6 attempts. Here's how it works:

Rules of the Game

  • You have 6 attempts to guess the correct word.
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Tile Colors Explained

Example: The hidden word is "CRANE".

You guess: "CLOUD".

C L O U D

  • Green: The letter C is in the correct position.
  • Gray: The letters L, O, U, D are not in the word at all.

You guess: "CRATE".

C R A T E

  • Green: The letters C and R are in the correct positions.
  • Yellow: The letter A is in the word but in the wrong position.
  • Gray: The letters T and E are not in the word at all.

Tips for Success

  • Start with words that contain common vowels (e.g., A, E, I, O, U).
  • Use the feedback from each guess to eliminate possibilities.
  • Pay attention to the position of letters marked in green or yellow.

Good luck, and have fun playing Wordle!
